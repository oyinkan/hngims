Frequently Asked Questions
What is HNG Internship about?
The Hotels.Ng internship program is a "remote" internship program aimed at grooming software developers and UI/UX designers. It runs several iterations. This is the sixth edition.
Who can apply for HNG Internship?
This is simple, anyone with the passion to learn regardless of the educational background, discipline, country and social status can apply.
What will the Interns be doing?
You're given tasks that are aimed at helping you hone your skills so as to become a better developer. Every task comes with a deadline. Might be weeks, a week, days or even hours. The interns will be working on real life projects for Hotels.ng, get paid for doing this and have a great chance to learn from the very best in Nigeria's tech space.
What Tracks are Available?
We have two tracks. The ‘Internship’ track and ‘Learning’ track. The learning track is an easy-going, unpaid track. The internship track is tougher. People who don’t complete their tasks get disabled from slack. Some people are here to just Learn, they will be on the learning track (unpaid). We will have others on the ‘internship’ track; who will be paid after 1 month. Then we have founders who are just observing, and can build relationships with upcoming coders
How do I know the track I belong to?
That's simple, depending on your current skill level. You must have the passion to learn firstly then you must be able to motivate yourself. Network with people that know, make friends with like minds and you will excel in your chosen track.
How does remote internship work?
Just like a normal internship, the only difference is that you don't need to meet physically to participate, therefore location is no barrier.
Do prospective interns need to speak a certain language?
All interested individuals just need to speak and understand English language as this is the general language spoken during the internship.
When can one find out if they have been selected for the internship?
The moment one has been selected a mail will be sent to acknowledge this.
Will certificates be issued upon completion of the internship?
Yes, Certificates will be issued upon completion of the internship, moreso its obtainable in PDF format.
Do I need to be a Pro developer to join the Internship?
No, you don't have to be a Pro developer to join. Some interns joined the internship without any background knowledge and now they are better at it because it's a continuous learning on the job.
Is there any Age limit considered for the Internship?
Nope. There's no age limit. Qualification does not count too. We are looking for those that possess, "The get things done attitude". If that's you, then you should apply!
What are the requirements to be an Intern?
You must own/have a PC (preferably laptop), have access to Electricity and Internet. Indicate /update your profile and most importantly obey instructions and complete tasks given to you.
Where can I find the tasks to be completed in the internship?
You can get updates in the Internship channel on Slack or visit the tasks page
What opportunities will I gain on finishing the internship?
You will gain the opportunity of working with mentors or other interns on paid projects and also have the opportunity to be employed by companies in need of your skills.
What if I want to learn another Programming language?
The internship will provide the opportunity to learn certain programming languages so you stand a chance of learning another language
I have a full time job and I really have interest in joining the internship,can I combine both?
Yes, you can have a full time job and still participate in the internship. You have to manage your time and know when to check the respective channels for announcement,lectures and tasks
